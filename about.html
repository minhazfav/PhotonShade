About PhotonShade

PhotonShade is a physics-inspired web tool that bridges the world of light, color theory, and quantum mechanics. It allows you to convert between standard digital color formats like HEX and RGBA, and a custom physical model:

Color ⇄ [hv (eV), Saturation %, Lightness %, Opacity %]

This tool is built for:

  • 🎓 Educators exploring the physics of color
  • 🎨 Designers curious about spectral purity
  • 🧑‍🔬 Developers creating simulations or educational content
  • ⚗️ Physicists visualizing Planck’s relation in a perceptual space

⚛️ Why Photon Energy?

At the heart of color lies light, and at the heart of light lies photon energy, described by the famous equation:

E = hv

Where:

  • E is the energy of a photon,
  • h is Planck’s constant,
  • v is the frequency of light.

In the visible spectrum, photon energies range from approximately:

  • 1.6 eV (red light, ~700nm)
  • to 3.1 eV (violet light, ~400nm)

🎨 How the Tool Works

1. Hue from Photon Energy

We calculate hue based on physical energy instead of arbitrary angles:

hue° = ((hv - 1.6) / (3.1 - 1.6)) × 300

This ensures:

  • hv = 1.6 → hue = 0° (Red)
  • hv = 3.1 → hue = 300° (Violet)

The last 60° (300–360°) are reserved for synthetic, non-spectral colors like magenta.

2. Saturation, Lightness, Opacity

  • Saturation: 0% (gray) → 100% (fully vivid)
  • Lightness: 0% (black) → 100% (white)
  • Opacity: 0% (transparent) → 100% (opaque)

🔁 Bi-Directional Conversion

  • HEX or RGBA → [hv, s, l, a]: See the energy equivalent of any digital color.
  • [hv, s, l, a] → RGBA: Design colors using quantum rules.

Most digital colors are abstract approximations. PhotonShade grounds color in physical meaning — where hue is driven by photon energy, not just artistic interpretation.

It's not just for aesthetics — it's a tool for understanding and exploration.
